Botswana Butchery: Situated on the waterfront, Botswana Butchery offers a sophisticated dining experience with a menu featuring premium cuts of meat, fresh seafood, and an extensive wine list.

Madam Woo: This vibrant restaurant specializes in Malaysian cuisine, serving up delicious dishes like roti canai, curry laksa, and beef rendang. Madam Woo offers a casual and lively atmosphere.

Fergburger: A Queenstown institution, Fergburger is renowned for its mouthwatering gourmet burgers. Despite its popularity, the lines can be long, but the wait is worth it for their flavorful creations.

The Bunker: Tucked away in a cozy setting, The Bunker offers a fine dining experience with a focus on seasonal and locally sourced ingredients. Their menu features a range of modern European dishes.

Accommodation:

Eichardt's Private Hotel: Located on the shores of Lake Wakatipu, Eichardt's is a luxurious boutique hotel offering elegant rooms and suites. It boasts stunning lake views and is renowned for its exceptional service.

Queenstown Park Boutique Hotel: Situated near Queenstown Gardens, this boutique hotel offers stylish and contemporary rooms. It features beautiful gardens and provides a relaxing atmosphere.

The Rees Hotel: Overlooking Lake Wakatipu, The Rees Hotel offers spacious and well-appointed rooms and apartments. It boasts a wine cellar, fine dining restaurant, and a convenient location close to the town center.

Sofitel Queenstown Hotel & Spa: Located in the heart of Queenstown, Sofitel offers elegant rooms and suites with a touch of French luxury. The hotel features a day spa, a restaurant serving French cuisine, and views of the Remarkables mountain range.

It's important to note that availability, rates, and preferences may vary, so it's recommended to check the latest information and reviews when making reservations for accommodation or dining.
